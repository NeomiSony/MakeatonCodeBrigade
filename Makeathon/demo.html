About Us

This is a platform to help students gain industry experience by providing them the opportunity to do real world projects. Industrial training embeds students with essential skills sets, experiences and knowledge. Theoretical and practical knowledge both are inseparable and important part of learning. Industrial training prepares students for future employment and opens the door of endless career opportunities. There are mentors to assist the curious and eager students. Students are also offered the opportunity to do projects from companies after they pass a certain criteria. There are various roles to teach and learn from one another.

Roles

Student

Students get the oppurtunity to be part of real life projects and on demo projects during their course of study thereby helping them in acquiring technical experience.

Mentor

Mentors are there to guide students on their projects.The mentor can either be a senior student who is eligile or teachers/alumni/parents.

Alumni

Alumni can always lend an helping to their fellow juniors.

Organisation

Organisations can also select students to get their projects done.
